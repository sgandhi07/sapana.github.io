Maintenance & Operations Perspective

Staging & Design Facilities Horticulture Other

This section displays some of the GIS work I did during my time with Friends of the High Line. A large part of my responsibility as Operations Data Coordinator was to develop the Park's first Maintenance and Operations plan. The maps in this section were created as visuzliations to supplement the text, which were Standard Operatinng Procedures and other guides and references.

Along with mapmaking and documenting the work that Operations Division was carrying out, I administered the Operations Divison database on PostgreSQL server, which informed these visualizations.

Most of the maps follow a style guide I established during my time with the organization.

A few maps do stray from the style guide, and those are my experiments with design.
