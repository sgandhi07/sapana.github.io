Energizing NYC

A couple map enthusiasts and I researched, conceptualized, and designed this map as part of "Atlas in a Day Challenge" hosted by Guerrilla Cartography Collaborative. The theme of "Migration" was announced at the beginning of day, and the challenege was to submit a map by end of the day; and below is our submission. I primarily supported the design part of the challenge by selecting the color scheme and scales.

The group of us agreed to a map a juxtaposition, because we realized immediately that there are two types of migrations: those that respect manmade political borders and those that don't.
